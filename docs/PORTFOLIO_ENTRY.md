## Title

D-ND UX-AI Seed

## Short Description

Reusable design seeds for agentic workspaces, public navigation, domain
templates, inspector surfaces and THIA-aware assistant UI.

## What It Contains

- Three-column workspace shell with persistent sidebars.
- Inner split panels with book-style collapse and mobile top/bottom resize.
- Megamenu seed based on the D-ND public site navigation model.
- THIA chat seed with focus awareness, drag, resize and full-screen mobile.
- Taxonomy, article diagram and response outline primitives.
- Copyable workspace template and portable agent skill.
- Integration checklist for internal teams and external adopters.

## Why It Matters

It gives D-ND projects and external teams a common way to build dense
human+AI interfaces without recreating layout, navigation, assistant context
and responsive behavior from scratch.
